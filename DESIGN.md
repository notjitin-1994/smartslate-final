# Design Context: Smartslate

## Color Strategy
**Committed**: Brand Teal (`#a7dadb`) and Indigo (`#4F46E5`) carry significant visual weight against a deep navy (`#020C1B`) background.

## Palette
- **Primary**: `#a7dadb` (Teal)
- **Secondary**: `#4F46E5` (Indigo)
- **Background**: `#020C1B` (Deep Navy)
- **Surface**: `#0d1b2a` (Navy Paper)
- **Text Primary**: `#e0e0e0`
- **Text Secondary**: `#b0c5c6`

## Typography
- **Heading**: 'Quicksand', sans-serif (Weights: 300-700)
- **Body**: 'Lato', sans-serif (Weights: 300-900)

## Elevation & Effects
- **Glassmorphism**: `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)`, `background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.02)`.
- **Glows**: Subtle teal/indigo glows for primary actions and highlights.
- **Borders**: Subtle `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)` for glass cards.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded (8px), gradient fills for primary, outlined for secondary.
- **Cards**: Large radius (16px), glass effects, interactive hover states.
